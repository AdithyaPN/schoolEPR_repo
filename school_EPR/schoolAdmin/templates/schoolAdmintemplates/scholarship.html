Bajaj Allianz General Insurance Company Limited is a joint venture between Bajaj Finserv Limited and Allianz SE. The reputation, expertise, stability, and strength of this alliance is the driving force behind Bajaj Allianz General Insurance. Bajaj Allianz received the Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority (IRDA) certificate of registration on 2nd May 2001 to conduct various businesses, including the Health Insurance business, in India. The company has an authorised and paid up capital of Rs 110 crores.Bajaj Finserv Limited holds 74% and the remaining 26% is held by Allianz, SE. As one of the leading general insurer of India, Bajaj Allianz General Insurance caters to individuals across demographics of the country and the corporate sector with its wide range of products, from personal accident to marine insurance and everything in between. Known for its claim and customer service levels, Bajaj Allianz has received iAAA rating from ICRA for eleven consecutive years, which indicates the company's highest claims paying ability and a fundamentally strong position. The company has a strong focus on customer centricity and aims to be an insurance provider delivering superior value with an excellent experience for the customer. The Company has been honored as the Best Insurer in India 2020 by IDC Financial Insights Innovation Awards. The CX Asia Excellence Awards recognised the company for providing Best Customer Experience 2020 & Best Brand Experience 2020. The company also won Gold Award at the prestigious Outlook Money Awards 2020 for the Non-Life Insurer Category. Additionally, the Company also received Best Travel Insurance award by Outlook Traveller and Best Motor Insurance Provider of the year award from Money today. As on 31st Bajaj Allianz continues to be one of the most financially robust general insurance companies in India by maintaining its growth as well as profitability. The company has registered a Profit Before Tax of Rs 1,078 crore and emerged as the most profitable insurer recording a Profit After Tax of Rs 728 crore. The company reported a GWP of Rs 7,687 crore that has grown by 30.3% compared to the last fiscal.
